# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 10 January 2026_

SAW Room Booking ("SAW", "we", "us") operates within the EU and processes personal data under the GDPR. This notice explains what we collect, why, how we share it, and the rights you have.

## 1) Who we are & contact
- Controller: SAW Room Booking.
- Email for privacy requests: privacy@saw.pt

## 2) Data we collect
- **Account**: name, email, phone, address, tax ID (NIF), role, verification status, password, optional 2FA secret/recovery codes.
- **Bookings**: room selections, dates/times, reservation history, applied cancellation policies, payment metadata (handled by processors).
- **Security & logs**: login/reset events, verification tokens, device/browser details, audit trails.
- **Files**: profile photos and room images you upload.

## 3) Why we process data (legal bases)
- Run the service and manage bookings (contract performance).
- Security: authentication, 2FA, fraud/risk controls (legitimate interest/security obligation).
- Account and booking notifications (contract performance/legitimate interest).
- Legal, accounting, and tax compliance (legal obligation).

## 4) How we use data
- Authenticate users, manage sessions, enforce access controls.
- Handle profiles, bookings, cancellations, and applicable policies.
- Send operational messages (reservations, changes, password/2FA flows). No marketing without consent.
- Maintain logs for security, diagnostics, and compliance.

## 5) Sharing
- Service providers for hosting and email delivery (subject to DPAs/confidentiality).
- Payment processors for transactions (we keep minimal metadata; processors handle card data).
- Authorities when legally required or to protect rights, users, and the platform.

## 6) International transfers
If data leaves the EEA, we use safeguards like Standard Contractual Clauses.

## 7) Retention
- Account data: kept while your account is active; soft-deleted accounts retained only as needed for legal/security/audit purposes.
- Security logs/tokens: retained for limited periods aligned with security needs.
- Booking and billing records: kept as required by law and accounting standards.

## 8) Your rights
Access, rectify, erase, restrict, or port your data, and object where applicable. Withdraw consent at any time. Contact privacy@saw.pt. You can also complain to your local data protection authority.

## 9) Security
- Enforced password auth with optional 2FA.
- Session/device checks and audit logging.
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS); no plain-text password storage.

## 10) Cookies
We use necessary cookies for login and session security. If additional cookies are used, you will be informed and given choices where required.

## 11) Children
The service is not directed to children under 16. Do not use the service if you do not meet your jurisdiction’s minimum age.

## 12) Updates
We may update this notice; changes take effect when posted with the new “Last updated” date. Material changes may be communicated in-app or by email.
